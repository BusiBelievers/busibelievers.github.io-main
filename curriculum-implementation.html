Balanced Workflow (Recommended)

  • Step 1: Identify track at intake: Program A (required behavioral) or Program B (voluntary curriculum/community support).
  • Step 2: Apply matching invoice type: Required Fee, Suggested Contribution, or Sliding Scale.
  • Step 3: Document quote/approval decisions with the quote comparison summary and attach supporting records.
  • Step 4: Before certificate issuance, confirm payment arrangements are documented or sliding-scale approval is completed.
  • Step 5: Capture outcomes in the evaluation form and keep all files tied to the case ID.
  • Step 6: Use founder dashboard for visibility control and public listing only when approved.
