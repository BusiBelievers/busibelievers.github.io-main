Sliding Scale Invoice

Features:
• Documents reduced amount
• Protects against disputes
• Hardship approval records

📋 Documentation & Compliance

Why This Matters: Proper invoice documentation is critical for nonprofit compliance, tax accountability, and dispute prevention. Each invoice type builds an audit trail that protects both BUSI and participants.

When officers, auditors, or agencies request documentation, BUSI can provide clear, legally compliant records that verify:

  • Program participation and fees
  • Payment arrangements or hardship status
  • Nonprofit tax-deductible contributions
  • Certificate issuance triggers and accountability
