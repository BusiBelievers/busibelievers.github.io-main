Busi Believers Incorporated is committed to strengthening individuals, families, and communities through mentorship, stewardship education, and practical assistance programs.

Ways to Support BUSI

Donations help support mentorship programs, community assistance projects, and volunteer initiatives.

Building partnerships with churches, volunteers, and community organizations to strengthen neighborhoods.
