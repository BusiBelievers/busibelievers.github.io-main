Support the Mission of BUSI

Busi Believers Incorporated is committed to strengthening individuals, families, and communities through mentorship, stewardship education, and practical assistance programs.

Ways to Support BUSI

Community Donations

Donations help support mentorship programs, community assistance projects, and volunteer initiatives.

Project Sponsorship

Sponsors can directly support community assistance projects such as home repair, vehicle repair, and property maintenance.

Volunteer Partnerships

Churches, organizations, and volunteers can partner with BUSI to support community outreach and mentorship efforts.

How Funds Are Used

Community Assistance

Supporting families and individuals with community service projects including home repairs, transportation assistance, and property care.

Mentorship Programs

Providing leadership development and mentorship opportunities for individuals working toward personal and professional growth.

Community Outreach

Building partnerships with churches, volunteers, and community organizations to strengthen neighborhoods.
