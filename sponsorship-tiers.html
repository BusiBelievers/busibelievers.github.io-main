BUSI Sponsorship Tiers

Structured sponsorship makes it easier for donors, churches, and partners to understand what their support funds and what outcomes they can expect.

Tier 1: Participant Support

$100
  • Helps cover curriculum materials or a participant scholarship gap.
  • Supports assessments, workbook access, and coaching administration.

Tier 2: Family Assistance

$250
  • Helps fund one community assistance case with quote review and follow-through.
  • Supports volunteer coordination and stewardship documentation.

Tier 3: Program Cycle

$500
  • Supports a cohort cycle, facilitator time, outcome review, and participant tracking.
  • Useful for church, agency, or partner sponsorship.

Tier 4: Mission Partner

$1,000+
  • Supports broader case assistance, curriculum delivery, and operational sustainability.
  • Ideal for recurring sponsors and institutional partners.

Sponsorship Reporting Promise

  • BUSI reports how support was applied to mission and service delivery.
  • Restricted and unrestricted support should be tracked separately in internal records.
  • Sponsors should receive outcome summaries, not private participant disclosures.
