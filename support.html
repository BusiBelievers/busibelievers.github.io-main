### Donation Transparency Policy Busi Believers Incorporated operates as a faith-based nonprofit organization committed to ethical stewardship, transparency, and community service. In some cases, individuals requesting assistance may contribute toward their own project or encourage friends and family to support their situation. All donations are received by Busi Believers Incorporated and are used to support approved assistance projects, volunteer efforts, and community service activities. Contributions made toward a specific project are not considered direct payments to an individual. All funds are administered and distributed by Busi Believers Incorporated according to project needs, volunteer availability, and organizational policies. Donations are used to support the mission of Busi Believers Incorporated, which includes mentorship, stewardship education, community assistance projects, and program administration. Busi Believers Incorporated maintains oversight of all projects to ensure fairness, accountability, and responsible use of donated resources.
