BUSI Program Operations Policy

Unified operating standard for intake, case handling, curriculum delivery, sponsorship stewardship, and closeout across BUSI programs.

Program Structure

Track Definitions

  • Program A: behavioral accountability, required participation, structured documentation.
  • Program B: faith and community restoration, mentorship, stewardship, voluntary or sponsored participation.
  • Community assistance cases may connect to either program when appropriate.
Role Standard

Role Separation

  • Founder/Admin: approvals, visibility, policy, and escalation.
  • Coordinator: scheduling, volunteer movement, case follow-through.
  • Facilitator: curriculum delivery, assessments, session notes.
  • Finance/Reporting: invoice integrity, sponsorship reporting, donor stewardship.

Case Lifecycle Standard

  • Pending Review: intake received and eligibility review underway.
  • Approved: accepted for service, waiting on scheduling or resourcing.
  • In Progress: active delivery, active service coordination, or active curriculum participation.
  • On Hold: blocked by documentation, funding gap, participant pause, or scheduling issue.
  • Completed: service or curriculum milestone finished and documented.

Only cases marked public and in Approved, In Progress, or Completed should appear on public-facing pages.

Required Documentation

  • Intake form and participant agreement before service begins.
  • Quote review when material assistance or outside vendors are involved.
  • Invoice type aligned to required, voluntary, or hardship context.
  • Before any certificate is issued, document payment arrangements or complete sliding-scale approval.
  • Use the Community Service Time Sheet for court-assigned service hour tracking and signatures.
  • Session notes and assessment evidence for curriculum participants.
  • Closeout summary with next-step or follow-up date.

Follow-Up Standard

  • 30-day follow-up for service stability and participant contact.
  • 60-day follow-up when ongoing accountability or referrals are involved.
  • 90-day follow-up for completion verification and outcomes reporting.
  • Escalate unresolved risks, repeated no-response, or safety concerns to leadership.
