Message From The Founder

Busi Believers Incorporated was founded with a simple but powerful purpose: to help rebuild the relationship between people and God while supporting individuals through their everyday struggles. Many people face difficult situations such as home repairs, vehicle problems, or financial hardship. These challenges can make individuals feel isolated or overwhelmed. Our mission is to remind people that they are not alone and that their voice — and their situation — will be heard. Through mentorship, stewardship education, and community partnerships, Busi Believers works to strengthen individuals and families while encouraging responsibility, faith, and personal growth. The BUSI program is built on four core principles: Building • Unity • Stewardship • Integrity Together with volunteers, community partners, and supporters, we aim to restore hope, strengthen communities, and help individuals move forward with dignity and purpose.

— Bernard Mitchell
Founder, Busi Believers Incorporated
