BUSI Community Stories

Stories and highlights from community service projects completed by Busi Believers Incorporated volunteers and partners.

Tree Removal Assistance

BUSI volunteers helped remove a dangerous tree threatening a San Antonio homeowner’s property. The project restored safety for the household and surrounding neighbors.

Lawn Care Community Support

A team of volunteers assisted an elderly resident with yard maintenance and cleanup. Community members came together to restore the property and provide encouragement.

Vehicle Repair Assistance

Volunteer mechanics helped repair a vehicle needed for employment transportation. Reliable transportation allowed the participant to maintain their job.
